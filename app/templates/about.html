About FLUX

Empowering Pakistan's energy sector with intelligent forecasting

Our Mission

FLUX is committed to revolutionizing energy management across Pakistan by leveraging cutting edge artificial intelligence and real time meteorological data. We enable organizations to understand, predict, and optimize their energy consumption patterns with unprecedented accuracy.

Technology Stack

Machine Learning

Advanced algorithms analyze historical consumption data and patterns to generate precise energy forecasts. Our models continuously improve with new data from your facility.

Weather Integration

Integration with Open-Meteo's free weather API provides real time temperature, humidity, and atmospheric data that directly impacts energy consumption patterns.

Data Analytics

Comprehensive analytics dashboards visualize consumption trends across weekly, monthly, and yearly intervals, enabling data driven decision making.

Enterprise Security

Your organization's data is protected with secure authentication, encrypted storage, and role based access control through our admin panel.

Real time Predictions

Get instant energy forecasts for your facility based on current weather conditions, historical usage patterns, and facility parameters.

Multi City Coverage

Full coverage of Pakistan's major and minor cities with precise geographic coordinates for hyperlocal predictions tailored to your region.
